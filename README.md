# 🚚 Turning Logistics into Loyalty: Delivery Delay & Customer Retention Analysis (Olist 2018)

Analyze how delivery delays impact customer satisfaction and retention in an e-commerce logistics system.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
Logistics is a critical pillar of e-commerce, especially in a large country like Brazil.  
This project investigates the hypothesis that delivery delays are strongly associated with lower review scores and reduced customer retention, particularly during the first purchase experience.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
- Identify key bottlenecks in the logistics process causing delivery delays  
- Analyze the impact of delays on customer review scores  
- Evaluate how first delivery experience affects customer retention  
- Understand customer sentiment through negative review analysis  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Python**: Pandas, NumPy (Data Cleaning & Manipulation)  
- **Visualization**: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Tableau  
- **Statistical Methods**:  
  - Funnel Analysis  
  - Cohort Analysis  
  - Sentiment Analysis  
  - Mann-Whitney U Test  
  - Spearman Correlation  

---

## 🔍 Data Analysis Workflow
1. **Data Loading & Merging**  
   Combine multiple datasets: orders, products, order items, customers, reviews, and payments  

2. **Data Cleaning**  
   Handle timestamp inconsistencies and logistics outliers  

3. **Feature Engineering**  
   - Order-level dataset  
   - First purchase experience dataset  
   - Customer-level dataset  

4. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**  
   Analyze delivery time distribution and regional performance  

5. **Statistical Analysis**  
   Perform hypothesis testing and cohort analysis to evaluate retention trends  

6. **Conclusion & Recommendation**  
   Translate findings into actionable business strategies  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- **92.3%** of orders were delivered on time  
- **7.7% late deliveries** had a **disproportionate negative impact**  
- Median review score dropped from **5 → 1** for late deliveries  
- Statistical tests confirm:  
  - Delivery delay is a **significant predictor of customer dissatisfaction**  
  - Strong correlation with increased churn risk  
- First purchase experience is **critical in shaping long-term customer retention**

---

## 💡 Strategic Recommendations
- Implement **proactive communication** for potential delays  
- Provide **early delay notifications** to manage customer expectations  
- Offer **light compensation** (e.g., vouchers) to mitigate negative experiences  
- Prioritize improvement in **transit stage bottlenecks**  
- Focus on **first-time customer experience** to reduce churn risk
